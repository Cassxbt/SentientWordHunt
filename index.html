      🎯 How to Play

      🎮 Objective

      Find Sentient-themed words to reach your target score before time runs out!

      🖱️ Controls

      Desktop: Click and drag to select letters

      Mobile: Touch and drag to select letters

      ⭐ Scoring

      • Sentient Words: 10 points (base)
      • Quality Sentient Words: 20 points (×2 multiplier)
      • Bonus Dictionary Words: 3 points

      📐 Rules

      • Letters must be adjacent (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal)
      • No gaps in letter selection
      • Words can go right, down, or diagonally
      • Reach target score before time runs out!

      🎯 Level System

      8 levels from Beginner to Legendary. Each level has:

      • Target score to achieve
      • Time limit
      • Minimum words to find
